What We Are?

Taxicabby is a Nigerian company that provides a digital means of transportation that is realiable, affordable and comfortable. It is a website that connnects drivers with passengers at their locations. The potential passenger just needs to order for a ride and enter their location and destination address and the website will find a nearby available driver that is registered with us.

Our Believes

Taxicabby, part of the Maxi Mobility holding company,
connnects private and business users with the forms of
transport that best suit their needs. With the aim of making
cities better places to live. We've created a sustainable
business mldel guided by ethical principles to help us act
responsibly. More than simply 'mobility as a service' (MaaS),
we seek to offer a future full of new possibilities for th3 40+
cities in Nigeria which we operate, respecting both the
environment and the people that live there.
