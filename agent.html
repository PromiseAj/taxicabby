How Taxicabby agent works

If you've got the skills, Taxicabby Agent platform provides you with the
tools. Recruit your 'leads' and add them on your Taxicabby agent
account. Help them complete various document checks to activate their
driver account. Give support and expertise as they get started as drivers,
and when they complete a certain number of trips, you will receive a
payout from Taxicabby.

As a Taxicabby agent, you can start small, but it's also easy to grow a
business fast. It's perfect for ambitiuos, community-driven people.

Note:

We'll ask you to provide documents including but not limited to: a copy of your national photo ID, bank statement, and profile photo when you sign up with Taxxicabby.
